Game Objective

Bet on the right horse and lead it to victory! The first horse to reach the finish line wins. But be careful... the setback mechanic can change everything at any moment!

How to Play

  1. Players bet on a horse.
  2. A card is drawn from the deck: the horse of that suit moves forward one position.
  3. The cards on the left mark the 7 stages of the race. When all horses reach a stage, the corresponding card is revealed.
  4. The horse matching that card's suit moves back one position.
  5. The horse that passes the seventh stage and crosses the finish line wins.

Special Rules

A horse can never move back beyond the starting point.

Setback cards are only revealed when all horses are on that stage or beyond.

Scoring System / Coins

Each horse bets 20 coins. If you win the race, you collect 20 coins from each player.

If you run out of coins, the game will gift you 20 coins so you can keep playing.

Game Modes

For now, you play solo! But don’t worry — multiplayer mode is on its way.

Game Options

  • Screenshot: After a win, you can save a snapshot of your victory directly to your device.
  • Sound: You can turn off music and sound effects from the settings menu if you prefer to play in silence.
  • Language: The game is available in Spanish and English. You can switch languages anytime from the settings.
  • Location: You may optionally allow access to your location for future personalized features. This is entirely voluntary.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can there be a tie? Yes. In that case, the horse with the fewest setbacks wins.

What happens if a horse moves back to the start? It cannot move further back. It will remain there until it advances again.
